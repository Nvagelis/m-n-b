Careers @ M&B

Make & Build harnesses the energy and skill of a diverse group of individuals to bring creative and technology together to transform our clients businesses.

We are contstantly evaluating talented individuals to join our team.

What you can expect:

  • Immediate opportunities to expand your knowledgebase and develop your technical/creative chops
  • Competitive salary, performance based perks, and solid benefits
  • A fresh MacBook with all the tools you need & 27" Thunderbolt Display
  • The Artery – Our relaxed workspace & creative lab positioned in the heart of Atlanta’s bustling Westside
  • Fun through unmatched camaraderie with an incredibly diverse team of people

Check out our current openings to see if there is a match.  If you don't see a spot but feel you are a good fit for our culture please send in your resume with a short note on why you feel M&B is the place for you.
