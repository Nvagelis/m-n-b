We are Make & Build

Make & Build is a performance tested team built to deliver impactful solutions. Our custom blend of creative and technology enables us to transform your business.

The development team has expertise operating in a variety of platforms from mobile and web applications to enterprise platform integration. Our creative team empowers us to provide a fit and finish that traditional development shops are lacking and allows us to meet the design standards of a digital agency.

Our mission is to stay on top of emerging technology as it hits the market. Through internal r & d projects we thoroughly evaluate new techniques so that we can create innovative solutions.

We are driven to provide deployment ready applications with eye grabbing designs for your organization. Using a transparent agile approach that keeps our clients informed in real-time, we control costs and generate tremendous value for the solutions we provide.
